Why Report?

On college campuses across the United States, one in five women and one in sixteen men are sexually assaulted. More than 80% of sexual assault cases on college campuses are not reported, which is a big problem. Most sexual offenders are repeat offenders which makes reporting any form of sexual assault extremely important. If you are a victim of sexual assault on a college campus, understand that you have plenty of options. Whether you choose to solely give information or to start a partial or complete investigation, know that you are not alone. Choosing to report could lead to justice, protection, and could start a movement to eliminate sexual violence on your campus. By reporting you are helping to raise awareness for sexual assault and helping other victims feel comfortable about speaking up. Help us help you by reporting a sexual assault below.
