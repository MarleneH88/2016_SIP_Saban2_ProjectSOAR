OUR CAUSE



    Many sexual assaults on campuses go unreported. If they are reported, not only does the person not get justice, but are made to feel like it was their fault in the first place. We want to challenge the status quo and help victims feel like they have a voice and feel safe. There are people who care about you and want to help you. There needs to be a change in how we deal with these problems. With our website, you can help make a change in the system.

Founders: About Ourselves

Hi there,

     Welcome to Project SOAR. Project SOAR was started by four high school girls who want to make the world a better place for sexual assault victims. We want to address this issue because college is supposed to be about start a new chapter of your life. However, all of that changes when sexual assualts occur. Sexual assault is rampant on college campuses, and with college on the horizon, we've set out to change the course of our futures and the futures of other soon-to-be college students.

A site for survivors

This is a site for survivors, advocates, and people looking to learn more about the sexual assault epidemic. On the site you will find

    1. Information about the problem,

    2. Ways that you can get involved,

    3. An innovative new way to help survivors report their assault,

    4. A place to share stories and find survivors like you.

Our mission is to help survivors find their voice, find support, find their community, and most importantly, find themselves again.

~Alyssa Furukawa, Marlene Hernandez, Amelia Andrews, and Elizabeth Gonzalez
